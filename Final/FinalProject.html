Lego Sets 1971 to 2015

Legos have become an iconic toy fascinating people of every age for years. Through the years there have been hundreds and thousands of Lego sets that have been released. We set out creating this visualization to show the history of Lego and how they have been influenced through the years. In the first visualization you can hover over each circle that represents a year which gives you basic information on that year. If you want to see more detailed on a year or years you can select them by clicking on the desired year or clicking and dragging to highlight multiple desired years. This will update the graphs below. The Top Themes bar chart is also clickable. You can get more detailed information on each theme by clicking on them.
